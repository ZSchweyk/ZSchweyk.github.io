Conference Sand Table

GitHub Repo


During my high school senior year at the Dos Pueblos Engineering Academy (DPEA), I worked on the software and hardware for a Conference Sand Table. The table was about 6-feet in diameter with an arm that spans the diameter of the table, with two marbles attracted to strong permanent magnets to create designs based off of polar equations. I used 3 ODrive motors (powerful servos), with one motor controlling the angular velocity of the diameter arm and the other two motors setting the distance of the two marbles from the center of the table.
